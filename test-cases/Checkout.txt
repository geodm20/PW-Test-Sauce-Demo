CHECKOUT:

TC11: Submit empty fields on checkout form 
Steps:
1. Log in with valid credentials.
2. In the product page, click "Add to cart" on a random number of available products.
3. Click on the cart icon.
4. Click on the "Checkout" button.
5. With empty form fields, click on the "Continue" button.
Expected result: An error will show up to state empty fields.

TC12: Complete checkout
Steps:
1. Log in with valid credentials.
2. In the product page, click "Add to cart" on a random number of available products.
3. Click on the cart icon.
4. Click on the "Checkout" button.
5. Fill the form fields and click on the "Continue" button.
6. Verify the price total and click on the "Finish" button.
7. Verify the order status and click on the "Back Home" button.
Expected result: Order is completed for selected products. Final price matches the sum of all listed products.
