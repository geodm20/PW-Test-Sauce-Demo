PRODUCT PAGE:

TC05: View all available products
1. Log in to navigate to the product listing page.
2. Verify that all products are displayed with their name, image, and price.
Expected result: All products are visible with name, image, and price.

TC06: Filter products by ascending price
Steps:
1. Log in with valid credentials.
2. Select the “Price (low to high)” filter from the dropdown.
3. Verify that the products are displayed in ascending order of price.
Expected result: The products are sorted from the cheapest to the most expensive.

TC07: Filter products by descending price
Steps:
1 Log in with valid credentials.
2. Select the “Price (high to low)” filter from the dropdown.
3. Verify that the products are displayed in descending order of price.
Expected result: The products are sorted from the most expensive to the cheapest.

TC08: View product details
Steps:
1. Log in with valid credentials.
2. Click on a product from the listing page.
3. Verify that the product details page displays the correct name, image, and price.
Expected result: The product detail page shows the correct product name, image, description, and price.
