SHOPPING CART:

TC09: Verify added products on cart
Steps:
1. Log in with valid credentials.
2. In the product page, click "Add to cart" on a random number of available products.
3. Click on the cart icon.
4. Verify the number of products on the cart page and the cart icon number.
Expected result: The number of detailed products matches the added products on cart.

TC10: Remove products from cart
Steps:
1. Log in with valid credentials.
2. In the product page, click "Add to cart" on a random number of available products.
3. Click on the cart icon.
4. Click on the "Remove" button of all listed products.
5. Verify the cart page status and cart icon number.
Expected result: No products are listed in the cart page and the number on cart icon dissapears.
